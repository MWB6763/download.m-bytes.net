Splashy Studio™

Splashy Studio™ is art software that lets you paint images or else edit them.

Download

Free w/ open source

 Read More…

Features

Painting

You can paint your own drawings, range the pen size, and change color.

Editing

You can edit image files on your computer by using filters.

Highlights

These are all highlights, so don't worry, there's more!

Custom Image Mode

With this mode, you can paint your own images, change color, and switch the pen size. You can also save your work (not completely) or delete it.

Edit Image Mode

With this mode, you can do the exact same thing, except there's no painting, but filters are added, and so is being allowed to import images from your computer.
